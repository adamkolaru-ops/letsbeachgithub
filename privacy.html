Privacy Policy

Who we are

This website is operated by Filip Rieger, trading as Let's Beach, based in the Czech Republic. You can contact us via our WhatsApp group or via the contact page.

What data we collect

We only collect data you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Your name
  • The message you send us
  • Your interest (type of training selected)

This data is used solely to respond to your enquiry and is not shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes.

Google Fonts

This website uses Google Fonts, a service provided by Google LLC. When you visit the site, your browser loads font files from Google's servers. This may result in Google recording your IP address and browser information. This is a standard technical process to deliver the fonts.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. If you decline cookies in our banner, Google Fonts will not be loaded.

Cookies

We do not use tracking or marketing cookies. We store only one item in your browser's local storage: your cookie consent preference (cookie-consent). This is not transmitted to any server.

Booking via Reenio

Training sessions and events are booked through Reenio (lets-beach.reenio.com), an independent third-party booking platform. Any data you provide during booking is governed by Reenio's own privacy policy.

Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via our WhatsApp group or through the contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version is always available on this page. Last updated: June 2025.
