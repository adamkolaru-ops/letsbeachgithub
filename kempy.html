frequently asked

What you need to know

What level are the camps suitable for?

Camps are for everyone – from complete beginners to advanced players. We always tailor the content to the group.

What should I bring?

Sportswear, sunscreen, water, and a good attitude. Equipment (balls, nets) is provided.

How big are the groups?

Maximum 6–8 players. Small groups are intentional – everyone gets individual attention.

How do I sign up?

Via Reenio or drop us a message through the contact form. Spots are limited, so don't wait.

Are there camps for kids too?

Yes, we run camps for both youth and adults. Age groups are always listed for each specific camp.

Where do the Prague camps take place?

On beach volleyball courts in Prague. Exact location is confirmed after registration.
