just you and me

Beach volleyball coach
Filip Rieger

I've been playing beach volleyball competitively since childhood, and now I teach it to others. I believe beach isn't just a sport – it's a way to move, have fun, and naturally improve your technique on the sand.

I coach so everyone gets what they need – whether you're just starting out or want to take your game to the next level. Good movement, solid technique, and great company are what matter.
