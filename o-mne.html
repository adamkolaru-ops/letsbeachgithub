My name is Filip Rieger. I've been playing beach volleyball competitively since childhood – I grew up on the sand and now I teach others to love it too. I believe beach isn't just a sport; it's a way of life: movement, freedom, great people, and fresh air.

I coach so everyone gets what they need – whether you're a complete beginner or a competitive player. What matters is movement, proper technique, and a good time. On and off the court.

Every session is tailored to the individual player. There's no one-size-fits-all template – there's your path, and I'm here to help you walk it.
